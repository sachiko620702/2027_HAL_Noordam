# USJ Day Checklist

## Before visit

- Studio Pass confirmed
- Express Pass confirmed if purchased
- Timed entry rules checked
- Super Nintendo World plan checked
- Donkey Kong Country plan checked
- App installed
- Battery and power bank ready

## Morning

- Arrive early
- Check app wait times
- Confirm timed entry status
- Prioritize Nintendo area if planned

## In park

- Use Express Pass according to time slots
- Track restaurant and snack breaks
- Keep rainy-day backup

## After visit

- Record wait times
- Record crowd level
- Record what worked
- Update USJ database
