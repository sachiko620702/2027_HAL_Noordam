# Pre-departure Checklist

## Documents

- Passport validity checked
- Flight booking confirmed
- Hotel booking confirmed
- Travel insurance checked
- Restaurant reservations confirmed
- USJ tickets confirmed

## Money

- JPY cash prepared
- Credit cards checked
- Credit card reward rules checked
- Tax refund rules checked

## Devices

- Phone
- Charger
- Power bank
- eSIM or roaming
- Maps saved

## Project sync

- Final itinerary updated
- Budget updated
- Reservation schedule updated
- Risk register reviewed
